About Me

My name is Ryan Cibart. I am from Vancouver, WA and I have a twin brother. I love soccer, video games, running, and hanging out with my friends. I am planning on heading to medical school after my time here and becoming an Emergency Physician. I cherish being at BYU and being around so many people who live like the Savior and help me learn more of Him!

Big lessons I am grateful to have learned recently:

  1. I can only control what I can control.
    • It sounds so simple, but I have found that I have a lot more peace when I only worry about the things I can influence!
    • It has been a huge boost to my testimony of Jesus Christ.
  2. God can make a whole lot more out of my life than I, or anyone else, can.
    • I don't blame Laman and Lemuel harshly for their lack of faith as I haven't always had the most faith, but I do chuckle reading their worries about Laban being more powerful than the Lord. He can do anything in an instant. The greatest of miracles!
    • I have seen miracles and I know I could not have done them only of myself.
  3. This isn't as recent as the others but other people can have a massive effect on my life.
    • I have been so blessed to have friends with great strengths that I admire and have worked to improve in myself.
    • There are things I didn't even know I could change in my life that I have been working on because of these friends!
